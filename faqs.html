Fasola-Discussions FAQ

This is a list of helpful information for subscribers to Fasola-Discussions. It supplements the information at the fasola.org website on the FAQ page there: http://fasola.org/community/lists/

Important Addresses

  • To post: fasola-discussions@googlegroups.com
  • To unsubscribe: fasola-discussions+unsubscribe@googlegroups.com
  • Fasola-discussions home: http://groups.google.com/group/fasola-discussions
  • Fasola communities FAQ: http://fasola.org/community/lists/
  • Fasola webpage: http://fasola.org

Scope of the List (from fasola.org)

The discussions list focuses on historical and technical aspects of shape-note singing. Examples of topics include tunebook history, shape note pedagogy, musicological analysis, as well as reviews of books, recordings, articles, and conferences.

Unsubscribing from Fasola-Discussions

Unsubscribing using email. To unsubscribe using email send a blank a message to fasola-discussions+unsubscribe@googlegroups.com. Note: it is very important that your unsubscribe message MUST be sent from the SAME EMAIL ADDRESS you are unsubscribing. That is, you can only unsubscribe your own address. If you have more than one email account, be sure you are logged on to the right one. If the account has been deleted, you'll need help from the moderator.

Unsubscribing using Google. If you have a Google account, it is very easy to manage your subscription at the website. Go to the Fasola-Discussions homepage at http://groups.google.com/group/fasola-discussions. Login to Google and click the link "Edit my membership" on the right side of the page. You should see an unsubscribe button along with other subscription options.

If you send a message to the moderator asking for help unsubscribing, you should include your email address in the message. The moderator will look up the address in the subscriber list and unsubscribe you.

If you still receive messages after unsubscribing, be aware that there are two fasola lists: fasola-discussions and fasola-singings. Make sure the messages you don't want to receive say "[fasola-discussions]" and not "[fasola-singings]" in the subject line.

Message Archive

The Fasola-Discussions email list is managed by Google Groups. You can view the message archive at the Fasola-Discussions homepage at http://groups.google.com/group/fasola-discussions.

Fasola-Discussions is a Public List

Anyone can view the messages posted to Fasola-Discussions. Only subscribers may post to the list, however. Fasola-Singings, in contrast, is a private list where only subscribers can view messages.

Replying to a Post

The message settings on Fasola-Discussions are set to Reply-To-Sender (as opposed to Reply-To-Group). So if you use the Reply button on your mail client, only the sender will receive the message. This is to prevent inadvertent posts to the list. To reply to the list, use Reply-All and the list address will appear in the message header. Or you can type or paste the list address manually, "fasola-discussions@googlegroups.com".

Managing Your Subscription Using Google Groups

Fasola-Discussions is operated through Google Groups. It is not necessary to have a Google account, but if you do have one you may use their subscription interface. If you go to the Fasola-Discussions homepage at http://groups.google.com/group/fasola-discussions you will see the login button at the top right corner of the screen. If you are already logged in, your username will be displayed.

When you subscribe to Fasola-Discussions, the default subscription type is to send each message separately to your mailbox. If you have a Google account you may change this to any of four subscription types. Go to the Fasola-Discussions homepage at http://groups.google.com/group/fasola-discussions, login to Google, and click the "Edit my membership" link to the right. You will see a menu "How do you want to read this group?" with four choices:

  • No Email (I will read this group on the web)
  • Abridged Email (Once per day or for every 100 messages)
  • Digest Email (Approximately 1 email per day)
  • Email (Send each message to me as it arrives)

Code of Conduct: Fasola Discussions

1. Purpose and Scope

The Fasola Discussions group exists to facilitate high-quality conversations regarding the historical and technical aspects of shape-note singing. We welcome singers, scholars, and enthusiasts to discuss:

  • Tunebook history and bibliography
  • Shape-note pedagogy and singing schools
  • Musicological analysis of tunes and texts
  • Discussions of relevant books, recordings, articles, and conferences
  • Other topics of general historical and technical interest

While we are a community of friends and singers, this list is specifically reserved for the topics above. General community announcements, strictly social chatter, or discussions about current Sacred Harp politics/governance are better suited for other venues. Singing announcements can be made to the Fasola Singings Google Group.

2. Expected Behavior

We aim to maintain a welcoming and intellectually stimulating environment. Members are expected to:

  • Be Civil and Respectful: Disagreements are natural and encouraged, but they must remain professional. Attack the argument, not the person.
  • Stay On-Topic: Ensure your posts relate directly to shape-note history, theory, or practice. If you are unsure if a topic fits, ask yourself: Does this contribute to our technical or historical understanding of the tradition?
  • Cite Sources When Possible: As this is a technical list, referencing your sources (tunebook editions, page numbers, historical documents) helps elevate the conversation.
  • Assumption of Good Faith: Assume that the other members of the discussion are acting in good faith, and extend grace to them and to the moderators.

3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated and may result in moderation:

  • Personal Attacks and Harassment: Bullying, insults, or ad hominem attacks.
  • Off-Topic Debates: Discussions concerning modern partisan politics, religion (outside of its historical context in the music), or commercial promotion unrelated to the genre.
  • Spam: Unsolicited advertisements or unrelated content.

4. Moderation Policy

This group is currently moderated by John Bealle and Will Fitzgerald. As fellow singers and volunteers, we aim to moderate with a light touch, but we reserve the right to:

  • Warn members who violate the code of conduct.
  • Delete posts that are offensive, spam, or significantly off-topic.
  • Remove members who persistently violate these guidelines or disrupt the community.

If you have a concern about a post or a thread, please contact the moderators directly off-list rather than engaging in a public dispute. Currently, all posts are reviewed before posting.

5. Copyright and Sharing

Please respect copyright laws when posting lyrics, sheet music, or audio. Brief excerpts for the purpose of analysis are generally acceptable (fair use), but please avoid posting entire copyrighted works without permission.
